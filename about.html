A bit about me

I spend a lot of time in front of a computer. So when I'm not working, you'll almost certainly find me outsise; be it year-round bike commuting, playing disc golf, or my true passion: whitewater river paddling! In addition to sitting on the board of directors I also instruct for a Minnesota-based non profit, Rapids Riders, so that others can be safely introduced to sport.

To disconnect and take in the scenery, I go on multi day rafting trips through places like the Grand Canyon of the Colorado River or the Green River through Dinosaur National Park.

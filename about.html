A bit about me

When I am not making maps, troubleshooting code, or otherwise in front of a computer screen I love being outdoors. Ideally that involves paddling, year-round biking, disc golfing, or camping.

Since 2013 I have taught whitewater kayaking and served on the board of directors for [Rapids Riders], a 501(c)3 nonprofit that promotes the responsible enjoyment of whitewater paddling and river stewardship. Simply put, I'm happy on the water for just a day trip or 21 days through the Grand Canyon of the Colorado River!

Of course, home-time is equally enjoyable and necessary at times. Luckily my wife enjoys boardgames, cooking, and craft beverages as much as I do.
